Example Keyword Research Prompts

Seed Keywords Prompt Example

Generate a comprehensive list of 50 seed keywords for a website in the "sustainable home gardening" niche targeting beginner gardeners. For each keyword:

  • Categorize by search intent (informational, commercial, transactional, navigational)
  • Indicate estimated search volume (high, medium, low)
  • Suggest potential content formats that would work well
  • Group into logical categories (e.g., tools, techniques, plants, seasons)

Focus on keywords that would be valuable for someone creating a new gardening blog aimed at urban apartment dwellers with limited space. Include a mix of broad terms and more specific phrases that would be less competitive.

Long-Tail Keywords Prompt Example

Create a list of 30 high-value long-tail keywords for a "digital marketing agency" targeting small business owners. For each long-tail keyword:

  • Provide the estimated search difficulty (easy, moderate, difficult)
  • Suggest a compelling title that would rank well for this term
  • Indicate the search intent behind the query
  • Recommend word count and content structure

Focus on long-tail keywords that demonstrate commercial intent where the searcher is evaluating services or considering hiring a digital marketing agency. Prioritize terms that would have lower competition but still meaningful traffic for a local or regional agency.

Topic Clusters Prompt Example

Develop a comprehensive topic cluster strategy for a "fitness equipment" e-commerce website targeting home gym enthusiasts. Create:

  • 5-7 main pillar topics with their primary keywords
  • 10-15 cluster subtopics for each pillar with relevant keywords
  • Suggested content types for each subtopic (buying guide, comparison, how-to, etc.)
  • Internal linking strategy between pillar and cluster content
  • Recommendations for featured snippet opportunities

Structure this as a complete content silo strategy that would help establish topical authority in the home fitness equipment space. Include both informational and commercial intent keywords to support the full buyer's journey.

Competitor Keyword Analysis Prompt Example

Analyze the keyword strategy of "competitor.com" in the "cybersecurity software" industry. Provide:

  • A breakdown of their apparent keyword targeting strategy
  • 20-25 high-value keywords they're likely targeting
  • Content gaps and keyword opportunities they're missing
  • Analysis of their content structure and how it supports their keyword strategy
  • Recommendations for how we could create superior content targeting the same or related keywords

Focus on identifying patterns in their content that reveal their SEO strategy, and provide actionable insights on how we can develop a differentiated approach that targets valuable keywords they may be overlooking or not optimizing effectively for.

Local SEO Keywords Prompt Example

Generate a comprehensive local SEO keyword strategy for a "dental practice" in [City, State]. Include:

  • 50+ local keyword variations incorporating neighborhood names, nearby landmarks, and surrounding communities
  • Service-based keyword modifiers (emergency dentist, cosmetic dentistry, etc.)
  • Question-based local keywords showing informational intent
  • Competitor-focused comparison keywords
  • Insurance and payment-related local keywords
  • Seasonal or special offer local keywords

For each keyword category, provide recommendations on which pages of the website should target these terms and how to naturally incorporate location modifiers without keyword stuffing. Include suggestions for Google Business Profile optimization based on these keywords.
