Blog Post SEO Prompt Example

Write a comprehensive, SEO-optimized blog post about "beginner yoga poses" targeting people new to yoga practice. The primary keyword is "beginner yoga poses" and secondary keywords include "yoga for beginners," "easy yoga poses," "morning yoga routine," and "yoga benefits."

The blog post should:

  • Include an engaging H1 title that incorporates the primary keyword
  • Have a clear structure with H2 and H3 headings that use secondary keywords naturally
  • Begin with an introduction that addresses the reader's pain points and establishes relevance
  • Include 7-10 beginner yoga poses with step-by-step instructions and benefits
  • Incorporate relevant internal linking opportunities
  • Include a FAQ section that addresses common questions about beginner yoga
  • End with a clear call-to-action
  • Be approximately 1,500 words to thoroughly cover the topic
  • Use a friendly, encouraging tone that's accessible to beginners

The content should satisfy informational search intent while providing practical value to readers who are looking to start a yoga practice at home.

Product Page SEO Prompt Example

Create SEO-optimized product description content for our "Ultra Lightweight Hiking Boots" product page. The primary keyword is "lightweight hiking boots" with secondary keywords including "waterproof hiking boots," "comfortable hiking footwear," "trail walking shoes," and "all-terrain boots."

The product page content should include:

  • An attention-grabbing H1 title that includes the primary keyword
  • A compelling meta description (150-160 characters) that encourages clicks from search results
  • 5-7 bullet points highlighting key product features and benefits
  • 3-4 paragraphs of descriptive content that naturally incorporates secondary keywords
  • Technical specifications section with material, weight, sizes, and other relevant details
  • Product usage scenarios that address different user needs (day hiking, backpacking, trail running)
  • Customer testimonial section that builds trust and incorporates natural keyword usage
  • Relevant product schema markup suggestions for enhanced SERP features

The content should satisfy transactional search intent while providing enough information to help customers make an informed purchase decision.

Keyword Research Prompt Example

Conduct comprehensive keyword research for a new website in the "home office furniture" niche. I need to understand the search landscape for creating SEO-optimized content.

Please provide:

  • 10-15 high-volume primary keywords related to home office furniture
  • 20-30 long-tail keyword opportunities with lower competition
  • 5-7 question-based keywords that we could target with FAQ content
  • Analysis of search intent for each main keyword category (informational, commercial, transactional)
  • Keyword grouping suggestions for potential website structure and content silos
  • Identification of seasonal trends or patterns in this niche
  • Competitor keyword gap analysis – what keywords are competitors ranking for that we should target?
  • Suggestions for keyword-focused content types (buying guides, product comparisons, how-to content)

For each keyword suggestion, include estimated search volume and competition level if possible. Focus on keywords with commercial intent that would help drive conversions for an e-commerce site selling home office furniture.

Competitor Analysis Prompt Example

Perform a detailed SEO competitor analysis for our skincare e-commerce website. Our top three competitors are [Competitor 1], [Competitor 2], and [Competitor 3]. We need insights to improve our SEO strategy and content approach.

Please provide:

  • Analysis of their top-performing organic content (topics, formats, length, structure)
  • Evaluation of their on-page SEO elements (title tags, meta descriptions, heading structure, image optimization)
  • Content gap analysis – what valuable topics are they covering that we're missing?
  • Identification of their primary and secondary keywords for main product categories
  • Assessment of their site structure, navigation, and internal linking strategies
  • Analysis of their backlink profile – what types of sites are linking to them and why?
  • Evaluation of their rich snippet and schema implementation
  • Recommendations for how we can differentiate our content and SEO approach

Focus on actionable insights we can implement to compete more effectively in organic search results for skincare products and educational content.

Content Audit Prompt Example

Conduct a comprehensive SEO content audit for our fitness blog. We've been publishing content for 3 years but have seen declining organic traffic over the past 6 months. We need a framework to evaluate our existing content and develop an improvement strategy.

Please provide:

  • A step-by-step process for auditing our existing blog content (approximately 200 articles)
  • Key metrics we should analyze for each piece of content
  • Criteria for categorizing content as: keep as is, update/optimize, consolidate, or remove
  • Framework for prioritizing content updates based on potential SEO impact
  • Guidelines for optimizing underperforming but high-potential content
  • Recommendations for content consolidation to address keyword cannibalization
  • Process for implementing redirects for removed or consolidated content
  • Content refresh schedule recommendations for maintaining relevance
  • Methods for identifying content gaps and new topic opportunities

Include suggested tools and resources for conducting this audit efficiently, and a template or framework we can reuse for quarterly content reviews moving forward.
