Local SEO & Google Business Profile Prompts

Generate powerful AI prompts to create Google Business posts, local landing page copy with geo modifiers, review request templates, and local citation content that boosts your local search visibility.

Your Local SEO Prompt:

How to Use This Tool

  1. Select your business type from the dropdown menu
  2. Enter your business name to personalize the content
  3. Specify your location to include appropriate geo-modifiers
  4. Choose the type of local SEO content you want to create
  5. Enter your primary service or product to focus the content
  6. Click "Generate Local SEO Prompt" to create your AI prompt
  7. Copy the prompt and paste it into ChatGPT, Claude, or your preferred AI assistant
  8. Use the resulting content to enhance your local search visibility

Example Local SEO Prompts

Google Business Profile Post Example

Create 5 engaging Google Business Profile posts for "Greenleaf Dental Care" in Portland, OR, focusing on their teeth whitening services. For each post:

  • Create a compelling headline (40-60 characters)
  • Write engaging body copy (150-200 characters) that includes location-specific references
  • Include a clear call-to-action
  • Suggest an image description that would complement the post
  • Incorporate seasonal relevance where appropriate

Create a variety of post types:

  • 1 post announcing a special offer or promotion
  • 1 post highlighting a patient success story (anonymized)
  • 1 post sharing an educational tip related to teeth whitening
  • 1 post about a community involvement or local event
  • 1 post featuring a team member or behind-the-scenes look

Each post should include relevant keywords for local SEO while maintaining a natural, engaging tone. Make sure the posts align with dental industry best practices and include appropriate hashtags where relevant.

Local Landing Page Example

Create comprehensive content for a local landing page for "Elite Plumbing Services" targeting customers in Scottsdale, AZ, focusing on emergency plumbing services. The content should include:

  • An SEO-optimized H1 heading incorporating the business name, service, and location
  • An engaging introduction (150-200 words) that establishes local relevance and addresses immediate customer pain points
  • 5-7 H2 subheadings with supporting content sections covering:
    • Emergency plumbing services offered in Scottsdale
    • Service area neighborhoods and landmarks
    • Response time and availability
    • Pricing and guarantee information
    • Local credentials, licenses, and insurance
    • Customer testimonials from Scottsdale residents
    • Local contact information and service area map
  • Natural incorporation of local keywords and geo-modifiers throughout
  • A locally-relevant FAQ section with 5-7 questions specific to emergency plumbing in Scottsdale
  • A compelling call-to-action focused on generating local leads

The content should be approximately 800-1000 words total, written in a conversational yet professional tone, and optimized for both search engines and conversion. Include recommendations for local schema markup to enhance local search visibility.

Review Request Template Example

Create 3 different review request templates for "Sunrise Yoga Studio" in Boulder, CO, to encourage clients to leave Google reviews. For each template, provide:

  • Email subject line options that encourage opens
  • Personalized greeting with merge tag placeholders
  • Brief, friendly body copy (100-150 words) that:
    • Thanks the customer for their business
    • Explains the importance of reviews to a local business
    • Makes leaving a review sound quick and easy
    • Includes a direct link placeholder to the Google review form
  • Professional sign-off
  • P.S. section with an additional incentive or information where appropriate

Create three distinct approaches:

  • Template 1: For new clients after their first class
  • Template 2: For long-term members who attend regularly
  • Template 3: For clients who completed a special workshop or event

Each template should feel authentic and not pushy, while still effectively encouraging reviews. Include guidance on the best timing to send each type of request and any follow-up recommendations.

Local Citation Content Example

Create consistent NAP (Name, Address, Phone) and business description content for "Mountain View Auto Repair" in Denver, CO, to use across local business directories and citation sites. Include:

  • Standardized NAP format that should be used consistently across all platforms
  • 4 different business description versions:
    • Short description (50-60 words) for directories with limited space
    • Medium description (100-150 words) for most citation sites
    • Long description (200-250 words) for primary directories like Google Business Profile
    • Social media bio version (150-160 characters)
  • Consistent business categories to use across directories (primary and secondary)
  • List of 10-15 relevant business attributes/amenities to include when available (e.g., "free estimates," "certified technicians")
  • Hours of operation in standard format
  • Service area definition (neighborhoods, zip codes, radius)
  • Payment methods accepted

All content should naturally incorporate important keywords for auto repair in Denver while maintaining a consistent brand voice and avoiding keyword stuffing. Include recommendations for which citation sites should be prioritized for this type of local business.

Service Area Pages Example

Create a framework for developing 5 service area pages for "Sunshine Landscaping," a landscaping company based in Miami that serves surrounding communities. The business wants dedicated pages for: Coral Gables, Pinecrest, Kendall, Doral, and Miami Beach.

  • For each service area page, provide:
    • SEO-optimized page title and H1 heading incorporating service + location
    • Introduction template (150-200 words) that establishes local relevance
    • 5-7 section subheadings that should be included on each page
    • Guidance on area-specific content to research and include
    • Local landmarks, neighborhoods, or geographic features to mention
    • Area-specific customer testimonial framework
    • Locally relevant FAQ structure (5 questions)
    • Call-to-action tailored to each specific area
  • Provide a strategy for:
    • Making each page unique while maintaining brand consistency
    • Naturally incorporating local keywords without duplication
    • Internal linking between service area pages
    • Local schema markup recommendations
    • Image optimization suggestions for local relevance

The framework should emphasize creating genuinely useful, unique content for each location rather than simply changing place names. Include recommendations for how to scale this approach if the company expands to more service areas in the future.
