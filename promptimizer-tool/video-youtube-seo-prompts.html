How to Use This Tool

  1. Select your channel type from the dropdown menu
  2. Enter your channel name to personalize the content
  3. Specify the video topic or subject you're creating content for
  4. Choose the type of video content you want to create
  5. Enter target keywords to focus the content (comma separated)
  6. Click "Generate Video SEO Prompt" to create your AI prompt
  7. Copy the prompt and paste it into ChatGPT, Claude, or your preferred AI assistant
  8. Use the resulting content to enhance your video's search visibility

Video Title & Thumbnail Text Example

Create 10 high-performing YouTube video title options for a smartphone review video on the "Tech Insights" channel. The video reviews the new Galaxy S25 Ultra. For each title option:

  • Create a compelling, clickable title (60-70 characters maximum) that balances SEO optimization with click-through rate
  • Include a suggested thumbnail text overlay (3-5 words) that complements the title
  • Provide a brief explanation of why this title would perform well

The titles should:

  • Naturally incorporate these target keywords: "Galaxy S25 Ultra review," "S25 Ultra camera test," "best smartphone 2025"
  • Use proven YouTube title formulas that drive clicks (questions, numbers, emotional triggers, etc.)
  • Avoid clickbait while still creating curiosity
  • Consider search intent for people looking for detailed smartphone reviews
  • Match the Tech Insights channel's professional but accessible style

Also provide recommendations for:

  • A/B testing strategy for these titles
  • How to analyze which title performs best
  • Best practices for thumbnail design that would complement these titles
  • How to update titles after publishing if needed for better performance

Include a brief explanation of how YouTube's algorithm evaluates and ranks video titles in 2025.

Video Description Example

Create a comprehensive YouTube video description for a 20-minute tutorial video titled "Complete Beginner's Guide to Yoga: 10 Essential Poses" on the "Mindful Movement" channel. The description should include:

  • An engaging first 2-3 sentences (approximately 300 characters) that will be visible before viewers click "show more" and that encourage viewers to watch the full video
  • A detailed breakdown of what viewers will learn in the video (150-200 words)
  • Timestamps for each of the 10 yoga poses covered, with the exact time markers left as [00:00] for me to fill in later
  • A brief instructor bio highlighting qualifications (50-75 words)
  • Call-to-action sections for:
    • Channel subscription
    • Related videos on the channel
    • Social media follows
    • Website/mailing list signup
  • Relevant hashtags (8-10) to include at the end of the description

The description should:

  • Naturally incorporate these target keywords: "yoga for beginners," "yoga tutorial," "how to do yoga poses," "yoga basics," "yoga at home"
  • Include one relevant affiliate disclaimer for yoga mat recommendations
  • Be formatted for easy readability with appropriate spacing and organization
  • Follow YouTube best practices for description SEO in 2025
  • Include one relevant question to encourage comments

Also provide guidance on how to update this description template for future yoga tutorial videos while maintaining SEO benefits.

Video Script Example

Create a complete YouTube video script for a 10-12 minute product review of a high-end blender for the "Kitchen Gadget Guru" cooking channel. The script should include:

  • A hook/intro section (30-45 seconds) that immediately grabs viewer attention and previews the value they'll get from watching
  • A brief channel-branded intro segment (10-15 seconds)
  • Main content sections covering:
    • Unboxing and first impressions (1-2 minutes)
    • Key features and specifications (2-3 minutes)
    • Performance tests with 3 different recipes (3-4 minutes)
    • Pros and cons analysis (1-2 minutes)
    • Price comparison with alternatives (1 minute)
    • Final verdict and recommendations (1 minute)
  • A strong call-to-action closing (30 seconds)

For each section, include:

  • Exact dialogue/narration text
  • Brief notes on suggested visuals/B-roll/demonstrations
  • Approximate timing
  • Places to naturally incorporate target keywords: "best blender 2025," "high-end blender review," "blender comparison," "is [specific blender model] worth it"

The script should:

  • Be conversational yet professional in tone
  • Include 2-3 moments of genuine personality/humor
  • Incorporate YouTube SEO best practices for viewer retention
  • Include 3 strategic places to remind viewers to like/subscribe
  • Feature one natural segue to mention a related video on the channel
  • Include one audience question prompt to encourage comments

Also provide brief guidance on how to adapt this script template for future product review videos.

Video Chapters/Timestamps Example

Create a comprehensive chapter/timestamp framework for a 25-30 minute YouTube tutorial video titled "Complete Guide to Adobe Premiere Pro for Beginners" on the "Edit Master" channel. The chapter structure should include:

  • 10-12 logical chapters that break down the tutorial into viewer-friendly segments
  • For each chapter/timestamp:
    • An SEO-optimized, descriptive title (maximum 40 characters)
    • A brief description of what content should be included in that section (30-50 words)
    • Approximate duration for each section
    • Any keywords that should be emphasized in that section

The chapter structure should include:

  • An engaging introduction (not labeled as "introduction" but with a more compelling title)
  • Logical progression from basic to more advanced concepts
  • At least one chapter focused on common beginner mistakes/troubleshooting
  • A brief but effective conclusion/next steps chapter

The chapters should:

  • Naturally incorporate target keywords: "Premiere Pro tutorial," "video editing for beginners," "learn Premiere Pro," "Premiere Pro basics"
  • Be formatted correctly for YouTube's chapter feature (00:00 format)
  • Follow optimal pacing for viewer retention (no section too long or too short)
  • Include at least one chapter title framed as a question viewers commonly search for
  • Balance comprehensive coverage with maintaining viewer interest

Also provide recommendations for:

  • How to reference these chapters in the video introduction
  • How to use these chapters in video SEO strategy
  • How to analyze which chapters get the most viewer engagement
