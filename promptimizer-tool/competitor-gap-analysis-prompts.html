Example Competitor Analysis Prompts

Content Gap Analysis Prompt Example

Conduct a comprehensive content gap analysis for a website in the "sustainable home products" niche. My top three competitors are GreenHome.com, EcoLiving.org, and SustainableHousehold.com. I need to identify content opportunities that will help me attract and convert environmentally conscious consumers.

  • Analyze the top-performing content topics and formats from each competitor (blog posts, guides, product comparisons, etc.)
  • Identify high-value topics that all three competitors cover but my site doesn't
  • Discover unique topics that only one competitor covers that are generating significant engagement
  • Analyze content depth, comprehensiveness, and update frequency patterns
  • Evaluate content structure, formatting, and multimedia usage strategies
  • Identify question-based content opportunities that competitors are ranking for
  • Analyze seasonal or trending topics that competitors have successfully leveraged

For each content gap opportunity identified, provide:

  • Suggested content title and format
  • Key points to cover that would make it more comprehensive than competitor content
  • Unique angle or perspective to differentiate from competitor content
  • Estimated search volume potential
  • Content production priority (high, medium, low) based on potential impact

The goal is to develop a strategic content plan that addresses gaps in the market while aligning with my goal of establishing authority in the sustainable home products space.

Keyword Gap Analysis Prompt Example

Perform a detailed keyword gap analysis for a digital marketing agency website compared to three main competitors: AgencyCompetitor1.com, AgencyCompetitor2.com, and AgencyCompetitor3.com. The goal is to identify high-value keyword opportunities that can drive qualified leads for our services.

  • Identify high-volume keywords that competitors rank for in positions 1-3 that we don't rank for at all
  • Discover keywords where competitors have featured snippets or other SERP features
  • Find keywords with commercial intent that at least two competitors rank for but we don't
  • Analyze keyword difficulty and competition levels for identified opportunities
  • Identify long-tail keyword variations that competitors might be overlooking
  • Discover question-based keywords that show informational intent in our niche
  • Analyze keyword seasonality and trending patterns

For each keyword opportunity, provide:

  • The keyword and its estimated monthly search volume
  • Current ranking positions of competitors
  • Keyword difficulty assessment
  • Search intent analysis (informational, commercial, transactional)
  • Content type recommendation to target this keyword
  • Priority level based on potential traffic and conversion value

Group the keyword opportunities into logical clusters that could be targeted with comprehensive content pieces, and provide a strategic implementation roadmap based on difficulty and potential impact.

Backlink Analysis Prompt Example

Conduct a comprehensive backlink gap analysis for an e-commerce website in the "premium kitchenware" niche compared to three main competitors: LuxuryKitchen.com, PremiumCookware.com, and ChefSupplies.net. The goal is to identify high-value link building opportunities to improve domain authority and organic rankings.

  • Analyze the backlink profiles of all three competitors, including total backlinks, referring domains, and domain authority metrics
  • Identify high-authority websites that link to multiple competitors but not to our site
  • Discover industry publications, blogs, and resource pages that represent link opportunities
  • Analyze the content types that attract the most backlinks for competitors (guides, tools, research, etc.)
  • Identify competitors' most linked-to pages and analyze why they attract links
  • Evaluate anchor text distribution patterns and strategies
  • Discover potential guest posting opportunities based on competitor backlinks

For each link building opportunity category, provide:

  • A strategic approach to acquiring similar backlinks
  • Content creation recommendations that would attract natural links
  • Outreach templates and strategies tailored to each opportunity type
  • Priority ranking based on potential SEO impact and acquisition difficulty
  • Metrics to track for measuring link building success

Develop a 3-month link building roadmap that prioritizes the highest-value opportunities while maintaining a natural, diverse backlink profile that will improve our competitive position in the premium kitchenware market.

On-Page Strategy Analysis Prompt Example

Perform a detailed on-page SEO strategy analysis for a health and wellness blog compared to three successful competitors in this niche: WellnessDaily.com, HealthJourney.org, and HolisticLiving.com. The goal is to identify on-page optimization opportunities that can improve our content's search visibility and user engagement.

  • Analyze the title tag patterns and strategies of top-performing competitor pages
  • Evaluate meta description approaches that generate high click-through rates
  • Identify heading structure patterns and keyword usage in H1-H6 tags
  • Analyze content length, depth, and comprehensiveness compared to ranking competitors
  • Evaluate internal linking strategies and anchor text usage patterns
  • Identify multimedia usage patterns (images, videos, infographics) and optimization
  • Analyze schema markup implementation and rich snippet opportunities
  • Evaluate page speed optimization techniques and Core Web Vitals performance

For each on-page element analyzed, provide:

  • Best practices observed from competitors
  • Specific optimization recommendations for our content
  • Examples of how to implement these improvements
  • Priority level based on potential ranking impact
  • Before/after examples to illustrate recommended changes

Develop a comprehensive on-page optimization framework that can be applied to both existing content and new content creation to consistently outperform competitors in search results for health and wellness topics.

Comprehensive Competitive Analysis Prompt Example

Conduct a comprehensive competitive analysis for a SaaS company offering project management software. Our main competitors are ProjectPro.com, TaskMaster.io, and TeamFlow.app. The goal is to develop a holistic SEO strategy that helps us gain market share and establish thought leadership in the project management space.

  • Analyze overall organic search visibility and traffic trends for all competitors
  • Identify keyword gaps across different stages of the buyer's journey (awareness, consideration, decision)
  • Evaluate content strategies, including topics, formats, depth, and publishing frequency
  • Analyze backlink profiles and link building strategies
  • Assess technical SEO implementations and site architecture
  • Evaluate on-page optimization patterns for top-performing pages
  • Analyze user experience factors that may impact SEO performance
  • Identify SERP feature opportunities (featured snippets, PAA boxes, etc.)

For each area of analysis, provide:

  • Key competitive insights and patterns identified
  • Specific opportunities where we can outperform competitors
  • Strategic recommendations with implementation guidance
  • Prioritization framework based on potential impact and implementation difficulty
  • Key performance indicators to track for measuring success

Develop a 6-month strategic roadmap that outlines the highest-priority initiatives across content, technical SEO, on-page optimization, and link building to systematically improve our competitive position in the project management software market.
